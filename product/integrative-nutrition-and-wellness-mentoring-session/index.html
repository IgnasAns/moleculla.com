Session: Integrative Nutrition & Wellness Mentoring (60 min)
Price: $130.00

A personalized, evidence – informed session designed to support prevention, longevity, and long – term vitality. This is not a one – size – fits – all plan – it's a strategic, whole – body approach that considers your nutrition, lifestyle, stress, and underlying health patterns.

Together, we assess key areas influencing your wellbeing – energy, digestion, hormonal balance, and resilience – and translate them into practical, sustainable habits. The focus is on early intervention and optimization: supporting your body before dysfunction develops, and building a foundation for healthy aging.

You will receive clear, tailored guidance rooted in holistic nutrition and clinical insight, helping you improve daily function while reducing long-term health risks.

What is included:

1) Pre-session

  • Health questionnaire (nutrition, stress, sleep, inflammation)
  • Goals

2) 1:1 mentoring session (60 min)

During the session:

  • Health history overview
  • Identify root causes (nutrition, stress, sleep, inflammation)
  • Discuss key systems (energy & metabolism, gut health, hormonal balance, nutrient status)

3) Personalised plan

  • Nutrition guidance (what to eat/ avoid)
  • Lifestyle protocols (sleep, stress, movement)
  • Simple weekly habits

4) Follow-up support
