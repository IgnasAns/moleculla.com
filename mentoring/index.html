Together, we assess key areas influencing your wellbeing - energy, digestion, hormonal balance, and resilience - and translate them into practical, sustainable habits. You will receive clear, tailored guidance rooted in holistic nutrition and clinical insight, helping you improve daily function while reducing long-term health risks.

What is included:

1) Pre-session
  • - Health questionnaire (nutrition, stress, sleep, inflammation)
  • - Goals
2) 1:1 mentoring session (60 min)
  • - Health history overview
  • - Identify root causes (nutrition, stress, sleep, inflammation)
  • - Discuss key systems (energy & metabolism, gut health, hormonal balance, nutrient status)
3) Personalised plan
  • - Nutrition guidance (what to eat/ avoid)
  • - Lifestyle protocols (sleep, stress, movement)
  • - Simple weekly habits
4) Follow-up support

Coaching and adjustments to support your progress as your habits compound.
